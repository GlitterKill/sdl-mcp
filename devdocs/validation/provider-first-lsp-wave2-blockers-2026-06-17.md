## SDL-MCP Hardening From This Pass

Several server failures timed out or closed transports while SDL was collecting provider facts. SDL-MCP now treats request timeout as the actionable failure and does not dispatch JSON-RPC cancellation through a potentially closed transport. Transport cleanup remains owned by `SemanticLspClient.dispose()`.
